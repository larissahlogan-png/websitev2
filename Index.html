The ALYRT Early Warning System: Three Pillars of Protection

ALYRT delivers high-fidelity alerts in three critical domains, linking financial distress directly to legal and operational risk factors.

Cash & Solvency Risk

Detects early signs of insolvency and potential reckless trading risk by analysing cash stretch against immediate payables.

  • 13-Week Cash Forecast: Real-time funding gap projections.
  • Liquidity Breach: Alert when current assets/liabilities ratio hits red lines.
  • Missed Bank Payments: Real-time flag from bank feeds.

Compliance & Legal Duty

Leverages IRD Gateway Services data to monitor breaches of tax law and fiduciary obligations in real-time.

  • IRD Arrears: Real-time status of GST, Income Tax, and PAYE debts.
  • Director Duty Flags: Specific alerts referencing the Companies Act 1993.
  • Plain English Remediation: Clear legal context and steps to resolve compliance gaps.

Operational Health

Identifies internal efficiency issues that lead to financial strain, before they affect the balance sheet.

  • Deteriorating Margins: Gross/Net margin drops vs. industry benchmarks.
  • Loss-Making Jobs: Flags projects or cost centres with negative profitability.
  • AR/AP Stretch: Alert when client payment terms or supplier debt is abnormally long.
